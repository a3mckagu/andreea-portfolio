The Brief

Background

A student engagement specialist referred me to the University of Waterloo’s Indigenous Relations Office to collaborate on an infographic-style project, applying my design expertise to create a resource later published by the Canadian Mental Health Association.

Stakeholders

Problem

Healthcare providers in Ontario lack culturally informed resources, contributing to disparities in Indigenous health outcomes. Health organizations in the Waterloo Region asked:

How might we design an infographic that informs physicians and provides actionable solutions while honoring Indigenous storytelling and art?
